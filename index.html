Brownies
Todays Recipe

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Serves 16 Brownies

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  2. 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  3. 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy
  4. 1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted if lumpy
  5. 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  6. 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  7. 2 large eggs
  8. 1/2 cup canola oil or extra-virgin olive oil
  9. 2 tablespoons water
  10. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

  1. Step 1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  2. Step 2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powderedsugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. Step 3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Step 4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  5. Step 5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan (it'll be thick - that's ok)
  6. Step 6. Use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached (note: it's better to pull the brownies out early than to leave them in too long).
  7. Step 7. Cool completely before slicing.*** Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. These also freeze well!
  8. Step 8. Enjoy
